PRAYER OF CONFESSION

Lord, we confess that our joy has not been in you.
We have allowed creation to take the place of Creator.
We have enjoyed the blessing, and forgotten the Blesser.
We have exalted the gift and neglected the Giver.
Forgive us for misplacing our joy,
Free us to find the desires of our heart in you.
Even so: come, Lord Jesus. Amen.
