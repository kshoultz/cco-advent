The season of Advent is a time to anticipate and celebrate the birth of Jesus Christ - God with us. This year our focus is the Kingdom of God. There was a time when all of creation groaned for the savior. The fulfillment of every promise, the incarnation of Creator God, the kingdom of heaven upon the earth. Every heart longed for His arrival - His Advent. Now more than ever, we yearn for His return, for Emmanuel to appear again, establish His Kingdom, and make all things new.

The life of Jesus, his death, and resurrection launched a kingdom characterized by HOPE, PEACE, JOY, and LOVE, and each week we want to celebrate God's story intersecting with those in our Church.

We invite you to join us here each day during the Advent season to reflect on what Jesus has fulfilled and long for His coming again. We are thankful for the privilege of reading Scripture with you as we look forward to the second coming of Christ.

But until then, may we pray over and over again: KINGDOM, COME.
